Airbnb NYC 房价预测 — 四种分析方法交叉验证

ALY 6015 · 小组项目

ALY 6015
用线性回归、假设检验、正则化和数据挖掘四种方法，分析2019年纽约Airbnb数据，预测房价走势。不是单一模型，而是方法论层面的交叉验证。

中文摘要

使用工具：R / Excel / Python

分析方法：线性回归、假设检验、正则化（Ridge/Lasso）、数据挖掘（聚类/关联规则）

分析对象：Kaggle 2019年纽约市 Airbnb 数据集（含 48,895 条房源记录，16 个变量包括价格、房型、位置、最少入住天数、评论数等）

核心问题：哪些因素驱动纽约 Airbnb 房价？不同行政区、不同房型的价格弹性如何？能否用四种独立方法交叉验证预测结果的稳定性？

结论要点：曼哈顿房价显著高于其他区（平均 $150+/晚），整套房源 vs 单间的价格差异达 2-3x。正则化模型在测试集上 R² 达到 0.62，优于基础线性回归的 0.51。四种方法一致指向"位置+房型"是主导因素。

Hypothesis Testing - Lizi Xian

The section will be a discussion of hypothesis testing. The specific method considered in this study is ANOVA, which is a test of hypothesis that is used to compare means of Airbnb price in New York city within more than two comparison groups. There will be two One-way ANOVA analysis performed using R programing language. The first assumption we premise is that there is a difference in price of Airbnb between 5 locations in New York city. Second, we hypothesize that there is a difference in price of Airbnb between the range of numbers of reviews. Next, Post-Hoc Analysis with Tukey’s test will be applied in the study in order to investigate more differences between comparison groups.

1. We wish to test whether there is a statistically significant difference in mean price of Airbnb among five locations in New York city.

μ1 = price of Airbnb in Brooklyn; μ2 = Manhattan; μ3= Queens; μ4 =Staten Island; μ5 = Bronx

Null hypothesis: H0: μ1 = μ2 = μ3= μ4=μ5

Alternative hypothesis: H1: Means are not all equal.

ANOVA analysis:

According to the result, the p-value associated with the test is less than significance level alpha, which is 0.05. Thus, we should reject the null hypothesis. We can conclude that there are differences in the mean price of Airbnb among five locations in New York City.

Tukey multiple comparisons of means 95% family-wise confidence level:

Three pairs with no significance difference in mean price

The rest of 7 pairs have significant differences in mean price.

As result, there are three pairs containing 0 in the 95% confidence level and adjusted p-value are greater than significance level alpha, which is 0.05. Hence, there is no significance difference for three pairs (Queens vs. Bronx; Staten Island vs. Bronx; Staten Island vs. Queens). However, the outcome also reports the rest of 7 pairs have significant difference in mean price.

2. We wish to test whether there is a statistically significant difference in mean price of Airbnb in New York city among three ranges of numbers of reviews.

μ1 = price of Airbnb from a smaller number of reviews (range: 0-5 posts); μ2 = price of Airbnb from a medium number of reviews (range: 6-23 posts); μ3 = price of Airbnb from a large number of reviews (range: over 23 posts)

Null hypothesis: H0: μ1 = μ2 = μ3

Alternative hypothesis: H1: Means are not all equal.

ANOVA analysis:

Since the P-value is 5.74e-11, which is less than significance level alpha= 0.05. Hence, we should reject the null hypothesis and conclude that there is a significant difference in price of Airbnb between ranges of numbers of reviews.

Tukey multiple comparisons of means 95% family-wise confidence level:

One pair with no significance difference in mean price

The rest of 2 pairs have significant difference in mean price.

According to adjusted p-values and the ranges of 95% confidence level from the result, there is one pair (Less reviews vs. Mid reviews) shows no significant difference in mean price of Airbnb. The adjusted p-value of the rest two pairs (Less reviews vs. Hot reviews; Hot reviews vs. Mid reviews) all less than 0.05 and the range of 95% confidence level does not contain 0, which indicates that there are significant differences in mean price of Airbnb among the rest two pairs.
